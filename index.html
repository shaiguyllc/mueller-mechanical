// Real Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is A2L refrigerant and why does it matter?
A2L refrigerants — including R-32 and R-454B — are the next generation of HVAC refrigerants mandated by the EPA starting in 2025. They have significantly lower global warming potential than older refrigerants like R-410A. They're classified "mildly flammable," which requires certified handling, specialized equipment, and updated installation practices. Mueller Mechanical is fully A2L certified, so our customers can upgrade confidently — now and as the regulations take full effect.
What are your service hours?
Our regular service hours are Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM – 5:00 PM. We do offer after-hours emergency service when your situation can't wait — a surcharge applies for calls outside regular hours. We do not offer 24/7 service by default, but we do our best to respond to genuine emergencies. Call 215-896-3665 to discuss your situation.
What brands do you install and service?
We are certified dealers and installers for four major brands: Amana, Daikin, Goodman, and Fujitsu. We also service most other major brands including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, and more. If you have a specific brand or system, call us and we'll let you know what we can do.
Do you service commercial properties?
Yes. We service commercial HVAC equipment including rooftop units (RTUs), commercial split systems, and custom commercial configurations. We work with small businesses, retail spaces, warehouses, and multi-unit buildings throughout Philadelphia and the surrounding area. Contact us to discuss your commercial needs.
What's included in a precision tune-up?
A Mueller precision tune-up includes a full system inspection, coil cleaning, refrigerant level check, electrical component inspection (capacitors, contactors, wiring), blower and motor check, thermostat calibration, filter inspection, and a written report of system condition. We'll flag anything that's at risk of failure so you can make informed decisions — no pressure, just honest assessment.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes — for new system installations, replacements, and significant projects, we provide free on-site estimates. For diagnostic service calls (troubleshooting an existing system), a service call fee applies, which is credited toward the cost of repair if you proceed. Use the quote form on this page to get started, or call us at 215-896-3665.
How do I qualify for the senior, military, or first responder discount?
We offer discounts for seniors (65+), active and veteran military, and first responders (police, fire, EMT). Simply mention your status when scheduling or at the time of service, and we'll apply the discount. Valid ID or documentation may be requested. Thank you for your service.
What areas do you service?
We service Philadelphia and the surrounding region, including Delaware County, parts of Montgomery County, and the Main Line. Key neighborhoods include South Philly, West Philly, North Philly, Germantown, Chestnut Hill, Fishtown, Kensington, Manayunk, Roxborough, Fairmount, Graduate Hospital, and Point Breeze. Surrounding communities include Upper Darby, Cheltenham, Springfield, Ardmore, Bala Cynwyd, and Bryn Mawr. Not sure if we cover your area?
